FAQ

Is Grax Client free?

Yes — Grax Client is completely free to download and use.

What platforms does it support?

Windows 10 and Windows 11.

Is Grax Client safe?

Yes. The client does not collect personal information and is designed to be safe on your system.

Does it work on all servers?

Some servers have rules about modifications. Always follow server rules and play responsibly.

How do I update?

Download the newest version from the website and replace your old files.

Troubleshooting

The client won't open

Ensure Java is installed and your antivirus is not blocking the file.

I get low FPS

Lower render distance, disable shaders, or enable FPS Boost.

Game crashes on launch

Delete your config folder and try again. If needed, reinstall the client.

Textures look broken

Check that your resource packs match your Minecraft version.

Modules aren't saving

Make sure the client has permission to write files in its folder.
